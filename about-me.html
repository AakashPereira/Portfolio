About Me

Accessibility and Usability Consultant

  • Company - AbilityNet
  • Time - August 2020 - Present

I started as an Intern Accessibility & Usability Consultant at AbilityNet during my second year of university, as part of the Professional Pathway scheme at City, University of London. This scheme allowed me to work part-time and also complete university on a part-time basis.

Diverging from the usual timeline, I was promoted early to the Junior Accessibility & Usability position whilst still studying at university. Once I graduated, I was promoted to the Mid-Level position. As part of my job, I complete website and app accessibility audits (including agile audit delivery), rechecks, and multi-language reviews against WCAG guidelines. I am also involved in working alongside the sales team to communicate with and identify the needs of clients to define work proposals.
